Welcome to TheCocktailDB

Cocktails are mixed alcoholic beverages that typically contain at least two or more ingredients, including spirits, juices, syrups, and bitters. They come in a wide variety of flavors and can be shaken, stirred, or blended to create unique taste experiences. From classic drinks like the Martini and Manhattan to modern creations like the Cosmopolitan and Mojito, cocktails have become a staple in bars and restaurants worldwide.
